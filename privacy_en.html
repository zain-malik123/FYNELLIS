PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy describes how users’ personal data is collected and processed, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – EU Regulation 2016/679).

Data Controller

The data controller is:
SOWERS BRIDGE LTD
Contact email (including GDPR requests): contact@fynelis.com

Personal Data Collected

In the course of using the website and the service, the following data may be collected:

  • Identification data: first name, last name, email address, phone number
  • Account data: login credentials, user preferences, alert settings
  • Technical data: IP address, logs, connection data, device type
  • Data related to the use of the service

No sensitive personal data within the meaning of the GDPR is collected. Payment card details are never stored by SOWERS BRIDGE LTD.

Purposes of Processing

Personal data is collected for the following purposes:

  • Creation and management of user accounts
  • Provision and operation of the SaaS service
  • Subscription and payment management
  • Delivery of service-related notifications
  • Security, maintenance, and improvement of the service
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory obligations

Processors and Third-Party Service Providers

Personal data may be processed by the following service providers:

  • Supabase: authentication and user database
  • Stripe: payment and subscription processing
  • OneSignal: notification delivery
  • Namecheap: hosting of the website

These providers act as data processors within the meaning of the GDPR.

Data Transfers

User application data is hosted within the European Union. Certain technical data related to the website may be processed outside the EU in the context of hosting services, subject to appropriate contractual safeguards.

Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Inactive accounts may be deleted after a prolonged period of inactivity.

Cookies

The website uses only strictly necessary technical cookies required for proper operation (security, session management, navigation). No advertising or marketing tracking cookies are used.

User Rights

In accordance with the GDPR, users have the following rights:

  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to object
  • Right to data portability

Any request may be sent to: contact@fynelis.com. A response will be provided within the legally required timeframes.

Security

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are implemented to ensure the security, integrity, and confidentiality of personal data.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated at any time to reflect legal, technical, or functional changes to the service.
